WHO ARE WE?

Moonbite is a monthly publication that combines art, writing, social justice, lifestyle and spirituality. It was created by Jasmine Pereira in 2020 with the goal to create a magazine that is a platform for new and emerging artists. It has grown into something with the intent to amplify voices for social change.

The name, Moonbite was created with the interest and importance of the horoscope and spirituality in mind in addition to the bite which references the strength of the publication being completely run by women.

Despite it being based in New York City, Moonbite seeks to go beyond the boroughs to feature artists and creatives from around the world.
